Impedance Compatibility: The TRIO15's 8Ω nominal impedance is ideal for the L-509Z. The amplifier's exceptional damping factor of 330 ensures tight bass control, which complements the open baffle's naturally extended low-end.

Sonic Synergy:

  • The Luxman's refined, slightly warm character pairs beautifully with the TRIO15's transparent open baffle presentation
  • LECUA-EX volume control preserves micro-dynamics at low volumes — ideal for the TRIO15's revealing nature
  • The high damping factor helps control the 15" woofers without over-damping the natural open baffle sound

Verdict: This is an excellent pairing. The Luxman L-509Z has more than enough power and current delivery for the efficient TRIO15 Classic, while its refined amplification circuitry (LIFES 1.0) will extract maximum detail from these revealing speakers.
