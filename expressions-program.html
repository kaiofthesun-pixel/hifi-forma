Overview

The Messages brand faced a critical decline in public perception–it was viewed as dated and uninteresting, with only basic functionality.

I developed and evangelized a new vision for Messaging, centered on augmenting self expression with the goal of building deeper connections.

This newly-resourced workstream was strategically timed with the release of the Google Pixel marketing launch, announced in 2024.

Screen Effects

I designed a system of fullscreen message reactions triggered by specific keyboard inputs (e.g., "I love you", "👎👎"). I established a unique visual language that used z-space and gradients to add depth to flat graphics, providing creative direction and collaborating with Oddfellows on execution.

Reaction Effects
Animated emoji

Worked with the Google emoji team to bring emojis to life inside Messages.

Photomoji™

Photomoji is a Google Message feature that allows users to create their own custom stickers or reactions based on personal photos.
