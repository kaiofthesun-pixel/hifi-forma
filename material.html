Overview

I was a founding member of the Material Design systems team, where we unified Google's fragmented product ecosystem for the first time. I contributed to core component and motion design.

Contacts app

I applied Material to the Android Contacts app while designing the entire app from the ground up. There, I pioneered motion patterns still in use today—specifically the adaptive header scaling that responds to user intent.

Outcome

The Material color system supports dynamic color, enabling personalization and accessibility while maintaining visual consistency across products.
