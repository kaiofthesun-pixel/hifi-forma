Summary

When LLMs first captured public attention in 2022, Google created several concepts to stay competitive with ChatGPT, Midjourney, and Dall-E in the messaging space.

Concepts proposed were based on image manipulation and the use of intelligence inside Messages to provide novel and delightful experiences.

Outcome
